What is Automated Assasins?

Automated Assassins is a 100% On-Chain P2E NFT MMORPG. The project will begin with the minting and staking of 3,333 Genesis Assassins. Holders will stake their assassins to earn $BOUNTY.

ABOUT

Automated Assassins is a 100% on-chain play-to-earn MMORPG and eco-system. Assassins and their mercenaries assassinate their targets, complete missions, and journey the cosmos. Players acquire better Armor and weapons, upgrade their safehouses, and compete in an immersive eco-system built on the Ethereum blockchain through interactive and strategic gameplay.

P2E Elements and Utility

Missions

Players must burn a set amount of $BOUNTY each time they begin a mission. The price depends upon the mission type. There are four mission types.

Assassinations

Take out wanted bosses and collect $BOUNTY and a chance to recover a Vault Key. Bosses change weekly.

Assault

Gather a squad and retake territory that has been overrun by outlaws and opportunists.

Supply Missions

Set out into the Assassinverse in search of helpful items and loot.
But be careful, any missteps could be lethal.

Patrol

Scour the wastelands of Rano for unclaim- ed $BOUNTY. Choose your own path in this RPG style mode through a series of options.

Items

Players may burn an item before beginning a mission to alter or boost their odds of success. Trackers, Recon Grenades, and Fusion Rifles may be used for Assassinations, while Repair Kits, Ammo Boxes, and Power Cores may be used on Patrol. These items may also be purchased from the Item Shop. Players have a chance of recovering a vault key from Assassinations, which will grant them the ability to mint a mercenary with their $BOUNTY.

$BOUNTY

Players will stake their Assassins to earn a daily sum of $BOUNTY. Staking Rewards are dependent upon Assassin Level. Level can be increased through successfully completing Assassinations or training in the Dojo. $BOUNTY is the lifeblood of the Assassinverse. It will be the primary token for all in-game transactions and is only produced by genesis assassins.

What is a MMORPG?
MMORPG stands for massively multiplayer online role-playing game, it's a type of video game that combines elements of role-playing games (RPGs) with the gameplay of multiplayer online gaming worlds. Popular examples of MMORPGS include games like World Of Warcraft and Runescape.
Why On-Chain?
NFTs are commonly reliant upon centralized servers or IPFS, meaning that if the owner of the IPFS stops paying their hosting bill the whole thing breaks down. On-chain NFTs are NFTs that are completely stored on the blockchain. This means that the metadata and image are stored “on-chain”, meaning that your nfts are not reliant on centralized servers or IPFS and will be stored on the blockchain forever. Our entire project is on-chain from images to game mechanics.
